The Project

College Event Management System

CEMS is a comprehensive web platform developed as a college capstone project by Rohit Kadam to address the fragmented, inefficient processes that colleges face when managing events across departments.

By unifying registration, communication, scheduling, and analytics into one platform, CEMS empowers both students and organisers to create more meaningful campus experiences.

Built using the MERN stack (MongoDB, Express.js, React.js, Node.js), the system is designed to scale from a single department to an entire institution.

Since its launch in 2024, CEMS has successfully managed 20+ events across 3 academic years, with over 4,000 student interactions recorded on the platform.

What Drives Us

Our Vision & Our Mission

At CEMS, every line of code is guided by a clear purpose — to transform how Abhinav College of Science and Technology organises and experiences campus life.

🔭
Our Vision

To be the digital backbone of every campus event across India.

We envision a future where no student misses an opportunity because of poor communication or disorganised processes. CEMS aims to be the trusted platform where every college event — from a small workshop to a national fest — is seamlessly managed, promoted, and remembered.

  • ✦ A unified platform for all departments and event types
  • ✦ Real-time data that helps institutions make smarter decisions
  • ✦ Scalable from a single college to a multi-campus university
  • ✦ Technology that celebrates and preserves campus culture
🎯
Our Mission

To simplify, digitise, and elevate the entire college event lifecycle.

Our mission is to eliminate the chaos of spreadsheets, WhatsApp groups, and paper forms that plague college event planning. We build intuitive tools that empower students, faculty, and organisers to collaborate effortlessly — so they can focus on what truly matters: creating unforgettable experiences.

  • ✦ Streamlined registration and ticketing in under 60 seconds
  • ✦ Instant notifications so no student misses an update
  • ✦ Transparent analytics for organisers and faculty
  • ✦ A joyful, inclusive experience for every student on campus

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register for an event? ▾
Go to the Events page, find your event, and click "Register Now". You'll need to be logged in to complete registration.
Can I cancel my registration? ▾
Yes, you can cancel up to 24 hours before the event from your Dashboard under "My Registrations".
How do I become an Event Organiser? ▾
Register with the "Event Organiser" role, then contact us via this form with your department and event proposal for approval.
Is CEMS free to use? ▾
Yes! CEMS is completely free for students. Some events may have a nominal entry fee set by the organiser.
